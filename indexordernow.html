Starbucks® Thanksgiving Blend is our expression of gratitude to
the world’s most distinctive coffee regions. Beans from Sumatra
in Indonesia, Sidama in Ethiopia, and the Antigua region of
Guatemala are expertly blended and richly roasted to create a
cup that’s delicately sweet—and worthy of the finest feast.
Elegantly flavorful and aromatic with notes of candied pecan,
sage and dried fig, it celebrates the true spirit of the season.
